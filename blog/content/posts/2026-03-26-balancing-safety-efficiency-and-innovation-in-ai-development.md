## Safety First: OpenAI's Safety Bug Bounty Program and Teen Safety Policies

OpenAI has launched a Safety Bug Bounty program to identify and mitigate AI abuse and safety risks. The program includes incentives for developers to report vulnerabilities, such as agentic vulnerabilities, prompt injection, and data exfiltration. Additionally, OpenAI has released prompt-based teen safety policies for developers using gpt-oss-safeguard, helping moderate age-specific risks in AI systems. These efforts demonstrate OpenAI's commitment to safety and responsible AI development. As developers, it's essential to prioritize safety and follow best practices to ensure your AI-powered applications are secure and trustworthy.

To get started with the Safety Bug Bounty program, visit the OpenAI website and learn more about the program's guidelines and incentives. You can also explore OpenAI's gpt-oss-safeguard library to implement teen safety policies in your applications.


## AI Efficiency Breakthroughs: TurboQuant and Model Compression

Google's TurboQuant project is redefining AI efficiency with extreme compression, enabling faster inference and reduced latency. By leveraging model compression techniques, developers can significantly improve the performance of their AI applications. OpenAI's Model Spec also serves as a public framework for model behavior, balancing safety, user freedom, and accountability as AI systems advance. These advancements demonstrate the importance of efficiency in AI development and the need for innovative solutions to optimize AI performance.

To explore TurboQuant, visit the Google Research blog and learn more about the project's objectives and methodology. You can also experiment with model compression techniques using OpenAI's Model Spec framework.


## Innovation in AI Development: Agentic Commerce and the Future of AI

OpenAI's Agentic Commerce Protocol is powering product discovery in ChatGPT, enabling richer, visually immersive shopping experiences. This shift from assistance to execution demonstrates the potential of AI to revolutionize commerce and customer experience. Additionally, the MIT Technology Review highlights the importance of truth and context in AI-driven commerce, emphasizing the need for transparent and accountable AI systems. As developers, it's essential to stay ahead of the curve and explore innovative applications of AI, such as agentic commerce, to create more engaging and interactive experiences for users.

To learn more about Agentic Commerce, visit the OpenAI Blog and explore the Agentic Commerce Protocol. You can also experiment with ChatGPT's shopping features to experience the power of agentic commerce in action.
