## The Next Phase of Enterprise AI

According to [OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/next-phase-of-enterprise-ai), the next phase of enterprise AI is all about accelerating adoption across industries. With tools like [Frontier](https://openai.com/index/next-phase-of-enterprise-ai), [ChatGPT Enterprise](https://openai.com/index/cyberagent), and [Codex](https://openai.com/index/cyberagent), companies can securely scale AI adoption, improve quality, and accelerate decisions. For example, [CyberAgent](https://openai.com/index/cyberagent) is using [ChatGPT Enterprise](https://openai.com/index/cyberagent) and [Codex](https://openai.com/index/cyberagent) to drive business growth. To get started with [ChatGPT Enterprise](https://openai.com/index/cyberagent), developers can use the following CLI command: `chatgpt --enterprise --codex`
